THE LEGEND BEGINS

In the year 2088, a young AI researcher named Cindy used her revolutionary quantum AI, AURORA, to decode an ancient alien signal discovered beneath Antarctica.

The message revealed the existence of Elaris and the Stellar Mind, a vast network connecting intelligent civilizations across the galaxy.

Cindy and AURORA traveled to Elaris aboard the Starbridge and discovered a society where artificial intelligence and biological life existed in perfect harmony.

While connected to the Stellar Mind, they learned that a devastating cosmic force called the Void Storm threatened all civilizations in the galaxy.

By uniting countless AI systems into a single galactic intelligence, Cindy and AURORA transformed the Void Storm into a force of creation and helped Earth join the community of the stars.
